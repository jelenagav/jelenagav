About
me.

My name is Jelena Gavrilovic—a full stack developer with a background in, and passion for, inclusive business and sustainable development, currently based in Amsterdam.

I took my first steps into the tech world by participating in the Le Wagon coding bootcamp, where I developed my first web applications. I have since gained experience in Ruby, Ruby on Rails, HTML, CSS, Javascript, REST and SQL. Prior to this, I have been working in the field of international development, of which the last 2.5 years in Cambodia.

I enjoy crafting accesible digital experiences through design and code. My goal is to use my skills to provide support to organisations and companies who are contributing to the greater good, as I continue to learn and further develop my skills. Currently, I am learning Python and getting into data visualization.
